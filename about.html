HooHoo was founded in 2016 to help people find great local bars in Corvallis, Oregon. The website was created for the purpose of a project for a University course named Computer Science/Database. Every effort was made to ensure that the content of this website is accurate. Almost all of the information here were from external site such as Yelp and Google. In addition, this website is not funded by any project grant, but rather from the other resources available to a university or college.
